Privacy Policy

Analytics

This site uses Umami Analytics, a privacy-focused tool that does not use cookies to track you. Umami collects only aggregated, anonymized data to help me understand website usage without identifying you personally. This approach aligns with GDPR requirements for data minimization and privacy protection.

Email Collection

If you choose to subscribe to notifications about new posts via our subscription form powered by Buttondown, you may provide your email address. Providing your email is entirely optional, and you may unsubscribe at any time using the opt-out or unsubscribe link included in every notification email.

Purpose

  • Aggregated analytics data helps us understand website traffic and improve the user experience.
  • Your email address will be used solely to send you notifications about new posts on this site.

Legal Basis

  • The use of aggregated analytics is based on our legitimate interest in understanding how the site is used (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)).
  • Email collection and processing is based on your explicit consent.

Data Sharing and Retention

  • Your email address will not be shared with any third parties.
  • Analytics data is anonymized and is not stored long-term.
  • Your email address is retained only as long as you remain subscribed. Upon unsubscribing, your email will be deleted from the records.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your email address from site records at any time.

To exercise any of these rights or if you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me.

More Information

For more details about Umami Analytics’ privacy practices, please visit their official website.
